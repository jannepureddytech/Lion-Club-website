JR. REDDY LIONS MULTISPECIALITY HOSPITAL

J R Reddy Lions Multispeciality Hospital is multispecialty, advanced and friendly hospital was established in September 2014 by Dr.J.Ramu Reddy & Dr.T.Mahathi, both a visionaries in health industry to deliver excellent healthcare solutions to the masses at a low cost with an aim of customer satisfaction.


J R Reddy Lions Multispeciality Hospital is a Govt Regd., 50 bedded hospital with economy, Pvt. & deluxe rooms. It’s well equipped with latest state of the art medical equipment like Digital XRay, computerized lab with auto analyzers, ICU with Ventilators and Modern labour room to deliver quality treatment.
